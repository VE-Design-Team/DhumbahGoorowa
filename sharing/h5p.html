H5P interactives

H5P is a great way to display content where word or images aren't enough, with the right pedagogy* these inteactives can support learning through activity and reflection.

The growing list below are H5P interactives the VEDT have built to date alongside relevant unit numbers that they're related to.

Each of these can be downloaded and modified to suit your needs with a free H5P account.

Embedding H5P activities into Canvas or digital content is as easy as embedding youtube videos.
